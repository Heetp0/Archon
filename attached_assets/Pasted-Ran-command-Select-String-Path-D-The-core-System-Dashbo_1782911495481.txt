The **Core Dashboard** is a comprehensive, interactive workspace hub built directly inside Obsidian using DataviewJS and Chart.js. It aggregates data, logs, and integrations across your entire system.

Here is a detailed breakdown of the functionality in each tab:

---

### 1. 🏠 Overview Tab
* **Current Focus:** Displays your active priority project and its current status at the top.
* **Key Stats Cards:** Tracks Active Projects, Code Projects, Research Items, Ideas, and overall Hours Logged (7d).
* **Hours Logged Chart:** A bar chart showing your daily overall logged hours over 7 or 30 days.
* **Recent Projects & Activity:** Lists recent projects with progress bars next to a chronological activity/agent log.

### 2. 📚 Study Tab
* **Study Metrics:** Displays total study hours over the past 7 days, your average daily hours, and your current study streak (consecutive days of studying).
* **Subject Breakdown Chart:** A horizontal bar chart parsing study hours across different topics (e.g., Mathematics, Solid Mechanics, Fluid Mechanics) from your daily notes.
* **Log Instructions:** Provides guidance on formatting daily note frontmatter (e.g., using `study-hours: 3` and `study-subjects: [...]`) to update the chart.

### 3. 📁 Projects Tab
* **Portfolio Health:** Summary cards classifying total projects into status categories: *Active, At Risk, Blocked, Complete*.
* **Hours Spent Over Time:** A line chart mapping the hours spent on standard projects (excluding coding/study) over 7, 30, or 90 days.
* **All Projects List:** Displays all project specs from `ProjectHub`, showing progress percentages, hours badges, and status pills.

### 4. 💻 CodeSpace Tab
* **Coding Activity Chart:** A line chart displaying your daily coding hours over the past 7 or 30 days based on `coding-hours` frontmatter.
* **Code Projects Directory:** Automatically tracks coding projects inside `Workspace/CodeSpace` (such as `archon`), showing their active completion progress, status, and total coding hours.

### 5. 🔬 Research Tab
* **Research Pipeline:** Lists and tracks all academic proposals, papers, and projects (`type: research-proposal` from `ResearchHub`), highlighting their current phases and status.

### 6. 💡 Ideas Tab
* **Idea Pipeline:** Tracks brainstorming items, pitch docs, and concepts (`type: refined-idea` from `IdeaHub`) to ensure interesting ideas are not forgotten.

### 7. ✉️ Mail Tab
* **Gmail Digest:** Lists your 10 most recent unread emails (sender, subject, and date), populated automatically using the `/briefing` command via Gmail MCP.
* **Direct Web Access:** Clicking any email row dynamically opens Google Mail in your web browser, pre-searched for that specific email.

### 8. 📋 To-Do Tab
* **Checklists Sync:** Displays your Google Keep checklist items synced via MCP (refreshed by `/briefing`), with completed tasks crossed off.

### 9. 📅 Calendar Tab
* **Week View Agenda:** Renders an interactive week-view calendar. It parses configurations from your `obsidian-full-calendar` plugin (or ical feeds) to display upcoming appointments, meetings, and timed events.
